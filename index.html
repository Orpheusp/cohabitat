COHABITAT

Build your co-living experience with intention.

Cohabitat is a free, cross-platform app that heps you sort out your household tasks in your co-living household, so that you can focus on the more important task:

Live and thrive with your housemates.

Schedule Household Chores

Set up rotational chore schedules for your household and alarms when it is your turn, so you never miss one.

Track Shared Expenses

Report shared expenses that you've covered for the house and get reimbursed, or reimbursed ones that your housemates have helped cover.

Use Cohabitat Household Debit Card ® to cover shared expenses and automatically split the bill (coming soon).

Vet Future Housemates

Check how compatible you are with any future housemates, and give a heads-up to your future housemates of your living preferences and expectations.

Schedule Household Chores

  • Set up recurring household chore schedules and reminders.
  • Track how well you have been keeping up with the chore schedule.
  • Qualify for certifications on your Cohabitat housemate profile for being a responsible housemate.

Track Shared Expenses

  • Report your shared expenses manually, or from electronic or paper receipts.
  • Set up reminders for recurring utility bills.
  • Turn on the "accountability" mode which adjusts your share of the expense according to how responsible you are as a housemate. As a great housemate who, for example, always complete your chores on time, you get a discount on split expenses!
  • * Use Cohabitat Household Debit Card ® to cover your household expenses. The bill is automatically split in the way you've configured, so you don't have to worry about covering expenses for your housemates who never pay you back (advanced feature, coming soon).

Vet Future Housemates

  • Keep track of all housemate candidates who you have met.
  • View candidates' housing profiles and check if they are compatible with you and your current housemates.
  • * Check how responsible the candidate is when it comes to fulfilling housemate duties with Cohabitat Synopsis ® (advanced feature, coming soon).
  • * Get a recommendation for houses and housemates based on your housing criteria (coming soon).
  • * Ask for renter references from the candidate's past housemates (coming soon).
